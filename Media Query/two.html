MS Dhoni, born on July 7, 1981, is a retired Indian cricketer and captain. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest cricketers and captains in the history of the sport. He led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2011 Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Dhoni is known for his calm demeanor, sharp decision-making, and exceptional wicketkeeping skills, earning him the nickname "Captain Cool". He also led the Chennai Super Kings to five IPL titles.
